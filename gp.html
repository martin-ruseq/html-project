General practice (GP)

    Dr. Margaret O'Hara

    MB BCh BAO, Diploma in Child Health

    Doctor Margaret O'Hara graduated at University College Dublin School of Medicine in 1996. After the intership at St James's Hospital, Dr. Margaret spend five years working in Spire Manchester Hospital. In 2001 Dr. Margaret came back to Ireland to continue the education at Royal College of Physicians of Ireland and graduate in 2003 for Diploma in Child Health. The IRFU member for over fifteen years, also has full medical registration with the Irish Medical Council. Dr Margaret O'Hara joint to Family Health Center in 2012. Dr. Margaret is a conscientious and reliable person and listens carefully to her patients.
