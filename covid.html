COVID-19

The health of our patients is our priority. During the pandemic, our clinic remains open and does everything we can to ensure your safety. Before entering, the client must decontaminate their hands. At Family Health Center, each arriving patient will receive a free FFP2 mask. Our clinic received Covid-19 Certificate which confirms that the Family Health Medical Center is safe.

If you have any COVID-19 symptoms, please contact your GP doctor at our clinic who will guide you step by step on what to do.


More information about COVID-19 at gov.ie and hse.ie


Our clinic provides vaccines against COVID-19. Our clinic uses vaccines from Pfizer. We vaccinate people of all ages. Vaccination takes place in two stages. The second vaccine is given two weeks after the first vaccination. Before taking the first dose, the patient will be tested for antibodies, if the test is positive, it means that the patient has already been ill and vaccination is not necessary. If the test is negative, the patient may be vaccinated. The job of the vaccine is to reduce the effects and complications of the virus.


First Vaccination: €50
Second Vaccination: €50

Our clinic also provides COVID-19 tests. Tests can be done in the clinic and on drive-through. We will perform two tests, the first test is a venous blood test for antibodies, and a genetic test from material from a nasopharyngeal swab. The patient receives the test result 24 hours after the test is performed. If the test is positive, our GP will contact the patient to give advice.



COVID-19 Blood Test: €40
COVID-19 Genetic Test: €40
