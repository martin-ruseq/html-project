Welcome to Family Health Center

    Welcome to Family Heath Medical Center. The Family Health Medical Center was founded in 1947 and is one of the oldest clinics in Ireland. We have over 70 years of experience and employs experienced doctors who are specialists in their fields. Our mission is to ensure the fastest possible recovery of our patients and provide them with the highest quality services. We pursue our goal through the continuous development of doctors and staff competencies and provideing GPs care for all ages. We also deal with acute and chronic diseases with particular emphasis on dental and visual health.

    The Family Health Center is a clinic located in Carlow providing a confidential and efficient service which deals with the needs of our patients quickly and accurately.
